Why I am interested in electronics

  • Posted on: 2017-01-03
  • By: Zixiang Nie
  • With:

　As Steve Jobs said “Stay hungry, Stay foolish”, I should keep plentiful energy and enterprising spirit in order to make a great achievement in the field of Electrical Engineering, and become a distinguished electrical engineer. This is my power of studying abroad, and I believe that your school will help me accomplish my goal.

　My interest in electronics dates back to the year of 1998. Although I was a little child at that time, I was extremely curious about how did the electronics work and what the functions they equipped with, so I used to throw myself into exploring their inner structures. Knowing what I was interested in, my parents always allowed me to play with their newly purchased electronics, such as electronic watch and BP beep pager. The magic power of electronic devices kept me spellbound for them.

　In the year of 2004, my passion for electronics, especially the communication network, was strengthened again when I owned my first computer. Compared with the bygone electronic devices, the computer brought me more pleasant. During that period, almost all my spare time was spent in investigating the functions of Windows, including the settings of control panel, the directory structure of Windows and the modification of registry keys, which deepened my cognition towards computer structure. In addition, I began to pay attention to the magazines and websites referring to communication network and computer knowledge like Netfriends. The extensive reading broadened my horizon greatly, as well as intensified my interest in electronics, even stirred up my belief to be an electrical engineer in the future.

　In the year of 2010, I bought my first smartphone and began my mobile internet life. I was so excited that I could search for the information and communicate with my friends by using it, even though it had a small screen size and incomplete functions. However, the emergence of iPod Touch in 2011 further perfected the using functions and realized the touch screen function. All of these let me enjoy the inventions brought by electrical technologies.

　It was not till I enrolled in the College of Electronic Information and Control Engineering at Beijing University of Technology in 2012 that I really had access to acquire the systematical electrical knowledge and witness that our life was more and more informational with the development of electrical technologies. In our campus, we could use a small card to eat in the canteen, shop in the supermarket, read books in the library, shower in bathroom and make an appointment for conducting experiments in the laboratory. Along with the accumulation of knowledge, I got to know all of these conveniences were brought by computer and electrical technologies. Therefore, I attended the elective courses of Embedded System, Database and Single Chip Microcomputer during my undergraduate study to hone my professional abilities and lay the foundations for my future researches in the field of electrical engineering.

　In short, I have never ceased my thirst for electrical engineering. I am looking forward to furthering my study in electrical engineering and preparing to plunge into the oncoming challenges. Internet of Things and Smart Home have become my interest of research after I read from the magazines and got in touch with some conceptual designs from the internet. The reason is that those innovations could serve the public and bring higher quality and efficiency to their life. I am ready to start off right here, right now!

Display field intensity data on Baidu map

  • Posted on: 2016-12-10
  • By: Zixiang Nie
  • With:

After gathered the data of field intensity of wireless signal, I considered how to make full use of the data. Inspired by Prof. Yang, I realized that the composition of our data resembles those of Sports & Running apps: we have both the coordinate of each point, the frequency and field intensity are correspond to something like speed or heartbeat. Thus, I installed a series of running apps and analyzed the way they show those data to the users. Notably, they have a week view or a month view which reminded that we should also take the date into consideration. In some extreme weather, the rains and winds could have effect on the signals.

Then, I learned from some book about data mining and data visualization, coded to show our data in different perspectives including maps and charts. Through our tools, our users could get some conclusions from the data easily.

Here is a demo of data on map.
Our test device have got the field intensity along a specifit route. From red to green, a color band is set to show the intensity from strong to weak.

You could:

  • scroll for zoom;
  • drag to move;
  • click the point and get further information
